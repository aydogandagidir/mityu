How it works

From conversation to approved report — on your device.

Three steps. Zero cloud dependency in the core path.

1

Record

Mityu captures system audio and your microphone directly on your machine. It never joins your call — no bots, nothing visible to other participants.

2

Transcribe locally

Whisper large-v3 or NVIDIA Parakeet runs on your hardware, GPU-accelerated where available. Fully offline — audio never leaves the device.

3

Review & approve

You get a source-linked draft: summary, action items, chapters, playback. Nothing is published or exported as final until you approve it.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is my audio uploaded anywhere?

No. Recording, transcription, and storage all happen on your device, and the app works with the network unplugged. Data only leaves your machine if you explicitly configure a cloud model with your own API key — and even then it is transcript text sent for summarization, never your audio. Analytics are off unless you opt in, and never include meeting content.

Does Mityu join my calls as a bot?

No. Mityu captures the audio already playing on your computer plus your microphone, so nothing joins the meeting and nothing appears in the participant list. A clear indicator is always visible while recording — and it is on you to follow local consent rules when you record others.

Which platforms are supported?

Windows 10/11 and macOS 13+ are fully supported, including system-audio capture (macOS asks once for the screen-recording permission — that is how the OS gates system audio). Linux is experimental and microphone-only for now, built from source.

Does it really work offline?

Yes — fully. Record, transcribe, summarize with local models, search, and export with no internet connection at all. You only need a connection for optional cloud models (bring your own key) and for update checks.

Which AI models does it use — do I need an API key?

No key required. Transcription runs locally with Whisper large-v3 or NVIDIA Parakeet. Summaries can run locally too, via Ollama or the built-in pipeline. If you prefer a cloud model, bring your own key for OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, or OpenRouter — keys are stored in your OS keychain, never in plaintext.

How do source links work?

Every AI-generated sentence and action item is bound to the transcript segment and timestamp it came from. Click any claim to jump to that exact moment and replay the audio. Items stay labeled “AI-generated · review required” until a human approves them.

Do I get updates after purchase?

Pro includes one year of app updates; after that, your license and your data keep working forever on the version you have. Business subscriptions include updates for as long as the subscription is active.

What is the refund policy?

Every purchase comes with a 14-day money-back guarantee. If Mityu is not for you, email support within 14 days and we will refund it — no questions asked.
