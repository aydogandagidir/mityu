FAQ

Questions, answered.

The short version: it runs on your machine, and you stay in control.

Is my audio uploaded anywhere?

No. Recording, transcription and — by default — summarization all run locally on your machine. Cloud LLMs are used only if you add your own API key, and transcripts sent to them are governed by your agreement with that provider.

Does Mityu join my calls as a bot?

No. Mityu captures system audio directly on your device. Nothing joins the meeting and nothing appears in the participant list — it works the same for Google Meet, Zoom, Teams or an in-person conversation.

Which platforms are supported?

Windows 10/11 and macOS 13 or later. Linux support is experimental and currently microphone-only.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Capture, transcription and summarization work fully offline with local models — Whisper or Parakeet for speech-to-text, Ollama for summaries. Internet is only needed if you choose a cloud LLM with your own key.

What is the refund policy?

Every purchase comes with a 14-day money-back guarantee. If Mityu is not for you, write to us and we will refund it.
